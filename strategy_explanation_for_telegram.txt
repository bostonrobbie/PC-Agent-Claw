15-MINUTE OPENING RANGE STRATEGY EXPLAINED

SIMPLE VERSION:
Trade the breakout of the first 5 minutes of market open, hold for max 15 minutes.

TIMELINE (9:30-9:45 AM ET):

9:30-9:35: CAPTURE THE RANGE
- First 5-minute bar
- Record HIGH and LOW
- This is your "Opening Range"

9:35-9:45: TRADE THE BREAKOUT
- If price breaks ABOVE high → GO LONG
- If price breaks BELOW low → GO SHORT
- Only ONE trade per day

9:45 AM: EXIT (Always)
- Close position at 9:45 bar open
- Max hold: 15 minutes
- Done for the day

EXAMPLE:
9:30-9:35: High=$19,500, Low=$19,450
9:37: Breaks above $19,500 → LONG @ $19,502
9:45: EXIT @ $19,520 → +$18 profit

WHY IT WORKS:
- Market open has highest volatility
- Breakouts show directional conviction
- Fixed exit = disciplined risk control
- 15 years proven profitable

RESULTS (15 YEARS):

- Max Drawdown: 21%
- Sharpe Ratio: 0.93
- Win Rate: 53.6%
- Trades: 3,515 (one per day)
